**Vrinda Cloths Store Annual Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)**

**Project Overview**
This project presents an interactive Annual Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze the sales performance of a clothing store for the year 2023. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Excel's data analysis and visualization capabilities.

The objective of this project is to help business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, understand customer demographics, and evaluate channel performance to support data-driven decision-making.

**Business Objectives**
- Analyze monthly sales and order trends.
- Compare purchasing behavior between men and women.
- Monitor order fulfillment status.
- Identify the top-performing states based on sales.
- Analyze customer age groups and gender distribution.
- Evaluate sales performance across different online sales channels.
- Build an interactive dashboard for quick business insights.

**Dashboard Highlights**
The dashboard provides insights into:

- Monthly Orders vs Sales Trend
- Sales Distribution by Gender
- Order Status Analysis (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded)
- Top 5 States by Sales
- Customer Age Group vs Gender Analysis
- Sales Performance by Online Channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Ajio, Meesho, Nalli & Others)
- Interactive slicers for Month, Sales Channel, and Product Category

**Key Business Insights:**
- Women contributed 64% of total sales, significantly higher than men (36%).
- Approximately 92% of orders were successfully delivered.
- Amazon generated the highest share of sales (35%), followed by Myntra (23%) and Flipkart (22%).
- Maharashtra emerged as the highest revenue-generating state.
- Adult customers contributed the largest share of purchases, particularly among women.
- Monthly sales remained relatively stable with peak performance during the first half of the year.
